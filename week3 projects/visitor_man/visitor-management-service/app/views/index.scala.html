Visitor Management Service

Combined visitor reception and processing service with direct Kafka publishing.

API Endpoints

  • POST /api/visitors/checkin - Check-in a visitor
  • POST /api/visitors/checkout/:id - Check-out a visitor
  • GET /api/visitors/:id - Get visitor details
  • GET /api/visits/active - Get active visits
  • GET /health - Health check
